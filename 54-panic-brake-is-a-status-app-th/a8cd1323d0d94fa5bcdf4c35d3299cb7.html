What a strong answer includes

01

Strategic Rationale

Clearly articulate the data-driven reasons why the existing global strategy was no longer viable and necessitated a shift.

02

Stakeholder Management

Explain how you identified key opposition points and used influence to align diverse global teams with the new vision.

03

Measurable Outcomes

Provide specific KPIs or impact metrics that validate the success of the pivot within a set timeframe.

Pro Tip

"The most effective pivots are those where you acknowledge the difficulty of the change while focusing on the long-term benefit for the organization."
