# Idea #17: Mood Tracker — Tap an Emoji to Log How You Feel

## Version: v1 (Original)
**Date:** 2026-03-27
**Status:** submitted

## Description

A simple mobile app where you tap an emoji to log how you feel each day. One tap, done. Over time it builds a mood calendar so you can see patterns — "I'm always low on Mondays" or "I feel great after gym days." No journaling, no long forms. Just an emoji and optionally a one-line note.
