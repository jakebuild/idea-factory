# Idea #41: Beauty-prediction app for tomorrow morning. Scan your dinner, dessert, or drinks and log your planned sleep, and the app predicts your next-morning Puff Score: puffiness, acne risk, dullness, and the exact time your face is likely to look camera-ready again. The loop is scan tonight, see tomorrow's damage, then follow one simple save-it action like extra water, an earlier bedtime, or skipping the salty add-on, so it feels like Cal AI for beauty anxiety instead of calories.
