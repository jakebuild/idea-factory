# Idea #28: Idea Swipe Tinder for ideas - swipe to decide BUILD or DROP. Browse through app ideas one by one like dating profiles. See the concept, tagline, and basic premise. Swipe RIGHT = Keep (build it later), Swipe LEFT = Drop (pass). Save kept ideas to a personal queue to review later. Feed refreshes daily with new ideas. Users can also SUBMIT their own ideas for others to swipe on. The more you swipe, the smarter the AI learns what you like building. Community voting determines which ideas get surfaced most. It's not just personal - the community decides what's worth building. Blocks: Swipe mechanic (from Tinder) + Collection (saved ideas) + Community voting + AI learning what you like.
