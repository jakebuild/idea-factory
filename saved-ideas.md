**Source:** mix-20260401-121305.md
**Targets:** Health
**Copies:** Cal AI — proved people will repeatedly scan meals if the payoff is an instant number reveal; Cal AI reportedly does $3M+/mo with photo-based food analysis
**Hook:** It doesn't count calories, it tells you whether your lunch is about to ruin your afternoon.
**Submit:** idea-factory submit "Meal scanner that predicts your afternoon energy crash instead of just calories. ## VibeCheck Voice
**Source:** mix-20260401-132058.md
**Targets:** Sex / Dating
**Copies:** Rizz — proved people will pay to avoid dating embarrassment when the payoff is an instant verdict on what to say next
**Hook:** It tells you what your crush hears when you think you sound confident.
**Submit:** idea-factory submit "Voice-note coach for dating. You speak the voice note, first-date story, or opener you are about to send, and the app scores how you come across: confident, needy, awkward, too eager, or actually attractive. It highlights the exact sentence that hurts your vibe, rewrites it in three tones, and lets you retry immediately so every recording feels like a before-and-after transformation."
