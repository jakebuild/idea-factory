# Idea #24: WeekPrint — pulls your Apple/Google Calendar and turns your week into a unique generative art poster. Each event category maps to a color, weight, and texture — a week full of back-to-back meetings becomes dense geometric grids, a weekend of free time becomes open watercolor washes. Every Sunday you get a new piece of art that is literally your week. One tap to save or share. The emotional hook: seeing your week as art reveals the texture of how you actually spent your time.
