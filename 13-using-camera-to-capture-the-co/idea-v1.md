## Description

### Critique
**Verdict: KILL IT | Score: 2/10**

This idea is already fully solved — Google Lens, the Amazon app, and Goodreads all do exactly this, and have for years. There is no differentiation angle, no data moat, no distribution path, and no retention mechanism. The problem is solved at the OS level. If interested in book discovery, find the gap these tools don't serve (e.g. in-store wishlisting, social "spotted this" sharing, or AI-curated YES/NO verdicts based on personal taste) rather than rebuilding a solved feature.
