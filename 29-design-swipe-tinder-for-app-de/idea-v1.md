# Idea #29: Design Swipe Tinder for app designs - swipe to BUILD or DROP. Designers submit their app mockups/screenshots. Developers swipe through designs: RIGHT = I'd build this, LEFT = Drop. See the actual design, not just text. Designers get paid when someone commits to build. Developers get designs they can actually build. It's a marketplace for pre-designed app concepts. Designers upload mockups (from Stitch, Figma, etc.), developers swipe through beautifully designed apps. When you swipe RIGHT, the design goes to your BUILD queue. The design includes all screens, flow, and specs. You can export it and start building. Designers earn when their design gets 'claimed'. It's like buying a template, but the creator gets credit. Connect to our inspiration database of 133 apps with 822 screenshots as seed content. Blocks: Swipe mechanic (from Tinder) + Design/mockup showcase + Build queue + Designer marketplace + Inspiration screenshots database.
