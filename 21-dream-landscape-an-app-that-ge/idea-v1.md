# Idea #21: Dream Landscape An app that generates a unique surreal isometric pixel art city based on your sleep data. Weather in the city = how well you slept. Time of day = when you woke up. Buildings reflect your dream patterns. Check your 'dream city' each morning like checking the weather - your subconscious manifested as a world you can explore. No two cities are alike.
