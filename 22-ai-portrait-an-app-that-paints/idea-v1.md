# Idea #22: AI Portrait An app that paints your daily selfie with AI in different artistic styles. Take a selfie every day → AI transforms it into art (oil painting, watercolor, anime, pixel art, etc.) → Builds a collection of your year as art. The app learns your face across time so each portrait connects to the last. Style changes daily or you pick. At year's end, you have a gallery of 365 unique paintings of yourself. Share your favorite portraits. The twist: your face stays recognizable but becomes art. It's not a filter - it's an AI painting of you.
