# Idea #23: Life Constellation — turns your weekly movement patterns into a personal star map. Places you visit become stars (brighter = more visits), paths between them become constellation lines, home is always the brightest star. No input needed — it maps automatically in the background. Each week's constellation is saved to your collection and shareable. The emotional hook: seeing your week as a constellation reveals how small or wide your world actually was.
