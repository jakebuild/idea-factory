# Idea #66: RelationshipDNA is a romance app that copies the Lasting model but applies it to single people analyzing their past relationships instead of couples working on their current one. You describe your last 3 exes in 2 minutes — personality type, how it started, how it ended. AI identifies your attachment style, the emotional archetype you keep choosing, and the repeating pattern across all three partners. Output is a shareable Relationship DNA card showing your pattern in a single visual. The reveal is immediate and screen-recordable: a bold pattern statement like 'You've been dating the same person three times without knowing it,' followed by a per-relationship breakdown showing exactly how the pattern played out each time. The loop is complete your DNA profile, share the card, and invite an ex or a friend to see if they recognize the pattern too. Monetize with a free pattern reveal and a paid tier that unlocks the full attachment style deep-dive, a compatibility filter for what to look for next, and a weekly reflection prompt to interrupt the pattern.
