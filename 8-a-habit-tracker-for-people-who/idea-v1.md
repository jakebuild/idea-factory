### Critique
**Score: 4/10 — NEEDS MAJOR WORK**

The insight is real (most habit trackers are guilt-tripping nag machines), but this submission is a tagline, not a product. The market is a graveyard of "better habit trackers," and "people who hate habit trackers" won't download another one without a concrete reason. No differentiator is articulated, no mechanics described, no customer discovery evident. The positioning is catchy — but there's no product under it yet. Needs: specific anti-patterns to kill, customer interviews, and one concrete differentiating mechanic before it's worth building.
