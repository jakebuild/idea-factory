### Idea 2: RelationshipDNA

**Targets:** Romance / Fear of Repeating Relationship Patterns

**Copies:** Lasting — $30M+ revenue, 2M+ downloads, proved couples pay $120/year for AI-powered relationship analysis; this copies the same model but targets single people analyzing their past instead of couples improving their present

**Blocks used:** Questionnaire data input - AI pattern analysis - shareable visual card reveal

**Concept:** You describe your last 3 relationships in 2 minutes — how long it lasted, how it started, how it ended, and a few words about their personality. AI identifies your attachment style, the emotional archetype you keep choosing, and the repeating pattern across all three. Output is a shareable "Relationship DNA" card visualizing your pattern in a single glance.

**Reveal moment:** "You've ended 3 relationships by emotionally withdrawing first. You consistently choose partners who need reassurance you can't give."

**The hook:** "It called out my exact pattern across three ex-descriptions and I've never felt so seen and unsettled at the same time."

**Solo ship check:** Yes — questionnaire + GPT-4 pattern analysis + card generation, no live data APIs needed. Hardest part is prompting for specificity so the pattern feels personally accurate, not horoscope-generic.

### Idea 3: VoiceScore

**Targets:** Attractiveness / Fear of Being Judged on First Impressions

**Copies:** Photofeeler — millions in revenue, proved people pay $3–5 per photo test to get their dating/professional photos rated before using them; this copies the "rate before you use it" model but applies it to voice

**Blocks used:** Microphone recording - AI audio analysis - multi-dimension score reveal with ranked feedback

Gives you a score across 4 dimensions (Warmth, Confidence, Clarity, Authority) vs others in your demographic, with a specific callout for what's dragging your score down the most.

**Reveal moment:** "Your voice scores in the top 20% for warmth but bottom 28% for confidence. Uptalk on declarative statements is the main factor."

**Solo ship check:** Yes — voice recording + audio feature extraction (pitch, pace, filler words) + GPT-4 scoring narrative. No special hardware or restricted APIs. Hardest part is calibrating dimension scores to feel differentiated enough that users believe improvement is measurable.
