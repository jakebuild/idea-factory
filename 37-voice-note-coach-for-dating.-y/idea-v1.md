# Idea #37: Voice-note coach for dating. You speak the voice note, first-date story, or opener you are about to send, and the app scores how you come across: confident, needy, awkward, too eager, or actually attractive. It highlights the exact sentence that hurts your vibe, rewrites it in three tones, and lets you retry immediately so every recording feels like a before-and-after transformation.
