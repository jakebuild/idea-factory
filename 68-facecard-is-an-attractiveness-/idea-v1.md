# Idea #68: FaceCard is an attractiveness app that copies the Photofeeler model but replaces the crowd with instant AI analysis so you get a scored First Impression Report in seconds instead of waiting hours for stranger ratings. You upload your LinkedIn or dating app profile photo and the app uses AI vision to score how trustworthy, competent, and likeable you appear in that specific image. The reveal is immediate and screen-recordable: a bold card showing your score across three dimensions plus the single highest-leverage fix — 'Your photo reads 6.1/10 for Trustworthiness — moving 6 inches closer to the camera would add ~1.4 points.' The loop is upload → get your score → follow one fix → re-upload to beat it. Each re-upload generates a new score so improvement is tangible and motivating. Monetize with a free one-time score reveal and a paid tier that unlocks unlimited re-uploads for A/B testing, a ranked breakdown of all three impression dimensions, and a side-by-side comparison card showing before/after scores that users can share.
