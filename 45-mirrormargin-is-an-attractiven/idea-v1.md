## Description
MirrorMargin is an attractiveness app that copies the UMax model but applies it to outfits instead of faces. You take 3 mirror selfies before a date, party, or interview, and the app ranks them by attractiveness, polish, and 'looks expensive' impression, then tells you the single swap most likely to lift your score, like changing shoes, removing a jacket, or fixing color contrast. The reveal is instant and screen-recordable: three looks side by side, one stamped Best First Impression, and one blunt reason the others lost.
