# Idea #42: Weekly hairline tracker for men who think they might be thinning. Take the same guided selfies each week and the app measures temple recession, crown visibility, and overall hairline change against your own baseline, then shows a blunt Hairline Loss Score and a 6-month projection if nothing changes. The loop is simple: scan, see proof of change or no change, follow one action for the next week, and come back because hair anxiety is not a one-time problem.
