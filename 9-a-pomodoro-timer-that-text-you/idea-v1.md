# Idea #9: a pomodoro timer that text your friend when you get distracted

## Version: v1 (Original)
**Date:** 2026-03-20 19:58
**Status:** submitted

## Description
a pomodoro timer that text your friend when you get distracted

### Critique
**Verdict:** NEEDS MAJOR WORK | **Score:** 4/10

The accountability partner angle has real psychological backing, but the idea collapses on two fatal problems: (1) you have no technically feasible way to detect distraction on mobile without platform restrictions killing you, and (2) the "friend" who receives texts has no incentive to stay in the loop and will opt out fast. The Pomodoro timer market is saturated, SMS costs money without a clear monetization model, and sending automated texts to phone numbers raises real TCPA compliance issues. The core MVP worth testing is simpler: skip distraction detection entirely and just send a daily summary to a friend — prove anyone cares before solving the hard problem.
